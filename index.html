Become a Web Developer

Web development is the process of building and maintaining websites. It involves a wide range of tasks, from designing the layout and structure of a website to implementing functionality and features. Web developers may use a variety of programming languages and technologies to create a website, and they often work closely with graphic designers and user experience designers to create an appealing and user-friendly website.
